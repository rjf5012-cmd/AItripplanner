Is AITripPlan free to use?
You can generate an itinerary without creating an account. In the future, AITripPlan may offer optional paid features for power users, but the core experience is designed to be simple and accessible.
Does AITripPlan book flights, hotels, or tickets?
No. AITripPlan focuses on planning and structure. You stay in control of all bookings and purchases, using your preferred travel sites or apps.
How accurate are the recommendations?
Itineraries are generated by AI based on general knowledge and patterns. They can be helpful for brainstorming and structuring your days, but they are not guaranteed to be perfectly accurate, up-to-date, or suitable for every traveler. Always double-check details like opening hours, seasonal closures, safety guidance, and local rules.
Can I rely on AITripPlan for safety decisions?
No. AITripPlan is not a safety tool and does not replace local authorities, official advisories, or your own judgment. You should always consult official sources and follow local laws and guidance when traveling.
What information does AITripPlan use?
To generate an itinerary, AITripPlan uses the trip details you enter on the planner form (like destination, trip length, interests, and constraints). The content returned is generated text based on those inputs and patterns learned by the AI model. For more on data handling, see the Privacy Policy.
Can I save or export my itinerary?
Right now, the simplest way is to copy the text and paste it into your notes app, document, or email. AITripPlan may add more export options in the future.
